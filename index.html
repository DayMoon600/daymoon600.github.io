Stack Bump: Hit Black Block

Stack Bump is a challenging arcade game based on reflexes and precision. Your mission is simple: guide the ball to the bottom of a tower made of moving platforms by smashing through the colored pieces.

But be careful — touching the black blocks ends the game instantly. Build up combos to activate Fever Mode and smash through everything without penalties.

With more than 100 exciting levels and increasingly difficult challenges, Stack Bump delivers fast-paced and satisfying gameplay perfect for quick sessions.

Sweet Day - Jelly Match 3 Games

Sweet Day is a colorful match-3 puzzle adventure filled with candy, jelly, and explosive fun.

Match three candies of the same color to clear them, create powerful special jellies by matching four or five, and complete exciting puzzle levels.

Challenge yourself across many sweet stages while aiming to earn three stars before running out of moves or time.

Word Fun Tap

Word Fun Tap is an addictive word puzzle game that challenges your vocabulary skills.

Swipe your finger across scrambled letters to discover hidden words and solve puzzles.

Easy to play but difficult to master, Word Fun Tap is perfect for players who love brain-training word games.

Pug Pop Bubble Shooter

Pug Pop Bubble Shooter is a fun bubble-popping adventure featuring adorable pugs.

Aim and shoot bubbles to match colors and clear the board while rescuing cute puppies trapped inside.

Enjoy relaxing gameplay, colorful levels, and endless bubble shooting fun.

Pug Pop Bubble Shooter 2

The adventure continues in Pug Pop Bubble Shooter 2. Rescue trapped dogs by matching and popping colorful bubbles in hundreds of fun levels.

Collect rewards, unlock special powers, and use powerful boosters to complete difficult stages.

A classic bubble shooter experience with cute pets and exciting challenges.
